# [DigiEXPploit]

## Description
Automates the process eating Digibanana's on the Digifarm to increase a Digimon's bond

Food reaction increases:
Ellipses: Bond grows by 0-1%
Music Notes: Bond grows by 1-2%
Heart: Bond grows by 2-3%

## Game Version
- Game: [Digimon Story: Time Stranger]
- Version: [1.000.004]
- Region: [US]

## ## Setup Instructions
1. Interact with the digimon that you want to feed and navigate to the Feed screen.
2. Ensure that your cursor has Digimeat select
3. Play the macro until you feel like you've increased your bond enough in accorance to food reactions above.
